So what are scholarships looking for?

Well-rounded candidates who possess soft skills such as leadership, communication, and teamwork; skills relevant to their field such as logical problem solving, and creativity, as well as excel in academics. A.K.A. People who are well developed in using all of the 8 cognitive functions (Te, Ti, Ne, Ni, Se, Si, Fe, Fi).

The 8 cognitive functions will be a useful tool to prepare for your interview.

Myth: you need to be an ENTJ/Te-dom girl boss (or boy boss) to obtain a scholarship.

Truth: Anyone has the chance to be a scholar. However, you need to show that you are well-balanced in using every 8 cognitive functions.

Before going into depth, make sure you understand what the 8 cognitive functions are (watch Frank James on YouTube)
