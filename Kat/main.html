Cat Living Rent Free in the School

This is the story of our school cat, Queen and an abandoned kitten.

Queen lived in our school for nearly a year, and that day while we were entertaining her, we noticed that in a dark, dark room that people no longer use, there was a soft meow. And the room was supposed to be locked all the time. But when we tried to enter the room, the room was surprisingly open. We went in and found a little, newborn kitten. It seemed scared and hungry. It hissed at us (spicy monster shiver me timbers) and only quieted down later when we brought it some food and water.

Queen then went in, they were not friendly to each other at first, but later on, they got along well. When Queen went out of the room, the kitten followed her timidly. The kitten was rather excited to see the light. There was a staircase nearby, and Queen went up the stairs. The kitten stumbled up and fell. After a few practices, the little kitten managed to jump up and down freely.

It was cute to see the two of them cuddling together sleeping. They were not bound by blood, but they look like a family. Maybe it was because that Queen gave birth to a bunch of kids twice, but she lost all of her kids :( (like literally lost, no one knows where did they go). Queen treated the little kitten as her own daughter. They lived in the dark together, and we went to see them frequently.

After a few days, when we went back to the same place, Queen meowed at us in an anxious tone. We looked around and heard meows from below. The kitten fell off the 2-metre-high staircase! We wanted to pick it up but the stairs were locked, and we could not get in. Queen pushed us gently towards the stairs, hoping that we could help to save the poor kitten. One of us sought help from a school worker to unlock the stairs to save the poor kitten. The kitten was not hurt, but it was frightened. We finally realised that this kitten could no longer stay here safely, as she might face the same fate as Queen's babies.

We decided to take the 2 of them to the vet for a check-up. We kept them in a box as if we were smuggling stuff lol, and of course, they struggled to escape like crazy, and we ran like crazy people on the streets to head to the vet.

The vet asked us their names, but we could not answer. The kitty's ain't got a name yet! (Queen did not have a name too at that time, we just called her pspsps lol)

We took the cats to a comfortable cat's bnb to chill for a little while, as our senior worked there as a part-timer. There were other cats around, and our Queen who was once a stray cat suddenly turned feral and fought with the cats there. So, we kept poor Queen in the box (jail time) to calm her down. We were thinking very hard about how to keep this poor little kitten.

Our senior advised us to consider it seriously. if we send it to [insert animal protection centre name here], it might get bullied by the other stray cats there. Maybe we could let it stay here for a short while? But exactly how long is a short while? What if nobody wants to keep it? Unfortunately, our parents would kill us if we take a stray kitten out of nowhere home. We thought of a lot of ways, but finally and luckily one of our friend's cousins was willing to keep it as a pet.

And so, the kitten finally found its new home.

Her new name is Snow White btw, check out her IG page:@snow.white0228.

When we brought Queen back to school, she seemed lost. She looked like she had lost her loved one. When we put her back at the place she liked to be, she would go back to the dark room, and search for the small little kitty. But the kitty was already gone. Queen meowed to us as if she was asking: where is my new friend? Where did you take her to? Why would you separate her from me? Am I not doing a great job to take care of her? But that is the only way to let you stay here and keep the kitten safe... We don't know if what we were doing was the right way.

Just like any other day, Queen continued to sit on our laps and sleep till the bell rang, cuddled towards us gently, and meowed whenever we brought her food.

After the whole lockdown chaos (March 2020 lol), when our school finally reopened after 3 months, Queen was nowhere to be seen.

She was gone.

Bad ending I know, but at least Snow White gets to live a healthy life :[
